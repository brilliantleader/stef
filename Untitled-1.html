My Dearest Halsin,

In the quiet moments when the world hushes its chaos, I find my thoughts drifting to you—your strength as unwavering as the oaks of the forest, your kindness as soothing as the rustle of leaves in a summer breeze.

You are a soul of profound beauty, a protector of balance, a being whose heart beats in rhythm with the wild. When you speak of nature's harmony, I feel as though I am hearing the song of the earth itself, and when you smile, it is as if the sun has broken through the densest canopy.

I am drawn to the way you carry yourself: a warrior when the moment demands it, yet a healer at heart, ever seeking to mend what is broken. Your devotion to the circle, your respect for all life, and your compassion for even the smallest of creatures inspire me to see the world with gentler eyes.

Though you are no stranger to pain or sacrifice, you wear your scars with grace, a testament to your resilience. It humbles me, knowing the burdens you have borne, and yet you remain unyielding, a pillar of hope.

If ever I have felt out of place or lost in the labyrinth of life, your presence has been a guide, steady and true. I yearn to be by your side, to walk among the trees with you, to share in your silences and your laughter.

Halsin, you are the wild and the wonder of the world wrapped in a mortal frame. And yet, it is the simplicity of your heart that moves me the most. With you, I am reminded that even amidst the storm, there is solace to be found in the arms of someone who truly understands.

May the seasons turn gently in our favor, and may we always find our way back to one another, no matter how far the paths may lead.

Yours always,
Stef

My Dearest Astarion,

There is a magnetism about you, a darkness interwoven with light that both entices and bewilders me. You are the night sky—beautiful and vast, hiding untold stories in your depths, and I find myself endlessly captivated by every facet of who you are.

Your sharp wit and the elegance with which you carry yourself are impossible to ignore, yet it is the vulnerability you shield so fiercely that truly steals my heart. Beneath the charm and mischief lies a soul yearning for freedom, for meaning, for love—and I long to offer you a haven from the shadows that have haunted you for so long.

I admire your strength, not only in battle but in spirit. To have endured what you have, to rise above it with your head held high, is a testament to the unyielding flame within you. You have shown me that even in the darkest of nights, there is beauty to be found, and that even those who walk the shadows deserve to bask in the warmth of the sun.

When you look at me with those piercing eyes, it is as though the world falls away, leaving only the two of us suspended in time. And when you allow yourself to laugh, truly laugh, I feel as though I am witnessing something sacred—a glimpse of the joy you so deserve.

Let me be your anchor, your companion, your solace. Together, we can carve a new path, free from the chains of the past. You are not defined by the darkness that shaped you, Astarion, but by the choices you make now, and I see in you the potential for something truly extraordinary.

My heart is yours, should you wish to take it. I will follow you through the shadows and into the light, wherever our journey may lead.

Yours always,
Stef
